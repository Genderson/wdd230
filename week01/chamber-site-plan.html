Reason why this name was selected

This name represent a beautiful city in my country where my parents were born and I used to visit in my school holidays. I want to do the chamber of commerce of this beautiful city to promote the commercein this city

Purpose

The purpose of the Boyaca Chamber of Commerce is to promote the formalization, strengthening and business innovation, as well as developing training activities in the commercial and industrial and others of interest to the city, through specialized courses, seminars, conferences and publications.

Scenarios

  • What communication channels does the chamber of commerce offer to conduct direct business without depending on intermediaries?
  • What are the training plans in the use of the technologies you offer?
  • Does the chamber of commerce have a membership directory that can be used to generate business?
  • What type of businesses show the greatest growth in the region?
  • What are the government aid that small and medium-sized businesses can receive?
  • What are the training areas that the chamber of commerce offers for small business owners?
  • What training plans does the chamber of commerce offer for entrepreneurs?
  • What type of business has the greatest growth projection within the next 10 years?
